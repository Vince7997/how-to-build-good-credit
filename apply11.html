How to Build Good Credit

To build good credit you need a long history of successful management of credit accounts and making on time payments. In order to do so, taking out a loan or having a credit card would be the first steps to building credit. The more accounts that you have the better, as long as payments are being made on time.

  • Take out a loan (credit card, installment loan)
  • Make on time payments
  • Increase the number of accounts you have
